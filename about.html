About Us

Local Marketplace is a project developed as part of the final year curriculum for the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSc IT) at Mumbai University.

About the Project

This website serves as a simplified e-commerce platform where users can browse products, add them to their cart, and place orders from local sellers. It demonstrates practical implementation of frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

Our Mission

The aim of this project is to support local businesses and explore the technical skills required to build a fully functional website interface. It also showcases how students can build useful solutions for real-world challenges.

What We Offer

  • Categories like clothing and footwear with real product images.
  • A responsive cart and checkout experience.
  • Easy order confirmation with customer details.
